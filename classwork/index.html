My Favorite Super Heroes

Hulk

Hulk is typically a nice man and brilliant scientist when he is docile. However, make him angry and he can turn into one scary "mofo".

Flash

Flash is so fast that he can most likely run around the world and punch himself in the back of the head.....if that was something he wanted to do.

Iron Man

Iron Man is an extremely smart man that has created a suit that allows him to accomplish most tasks such as flying, shooting rockets off of his arm, and basically being a total badass.
